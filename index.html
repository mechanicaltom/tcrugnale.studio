Mechanical 4 Axis CNC Milling

Silicone Mold Tooling

The brief

To produce a silicone rubber component that would attach to a hermetic enclosure for a neurotech application.

What I built

A mould tool machined from PTFE (Teflon) using 4-axis CNC — chosen for its biocompatibility, chemical resistance, and the ability to machine complex cavity geometry rapidly without secondary finishing.

The tool was designed in SolidWorks using the cavity tool workflow and programmed in Fusion 360's advanced manufacturing package. Used in conjunction with a transfer press, the tooling produces bubble-free parts consistently straight from the tool.
